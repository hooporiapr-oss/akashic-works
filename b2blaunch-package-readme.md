## ğŸ“ Sales Training

### Demo Script (30 minutes)

**Minutes 1-5: Introduction**
- Thank them for their time
- Learn about their facility (# residents, current programs, pain points)
- Set agenda for demo

**Minutes 6-15: Live Platform Demo**
- Show all 6 programs (focus on 2-3 favorites)
- Demonstrate bilingual toggle
- Show difficulty scaling
- Let them try it hands-on

**Minutes 16-23: Admin Dashboard**
- Individual resident tracking
- Progress reports
- Export features
- Bulk user creation (Standard/Premium)

**Minutes 24-28: Pricing & ROI**
- Show pricing sheet
- Calculate their specific savings
- Explain implementation timeline (1-2 weeks)

**Minutes 29-30: Next Steps**
- Offer 30-day free trial
- Answer questions
- Schedule follow-up if needed

### Qualifying Questions
1. How many residents do you currently have?
2. What cognitive activities do you currently offer?
3. What's your annual budget for cognitive therapy/activities?
4. Who makes the purchasing decision?
5. What's your decision timeline?

### Red Flags (Disqualify)
- Under 10 residents (too small)
- Zero budget allocated
- No decision-maker access
- Not interested in cognitive care
